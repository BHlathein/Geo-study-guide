  1. According to the Principle of Superposition

  2. Fossils can be used to date the ________ ages of rocks.

    • a. physical weathering
    • b. chemical weathering

    • a. wells in unconfined aquifers
    • b. aquitards

    • a. because that is where mud is usually deposited and where the clams live
    • b. because the barrier islands are moving towards the mainland. The island overrides the deposits of mud formed behind the island, and these old mud deposits are eventually exposed again on the seaward side of the island

    • d. because some kid carried a little plastic bucket full of mud and clam shells to the beach and dumped them there
